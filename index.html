Our Pipeline

From script to screen.

Three stages, no shortcuts. Every Rayrity Films production moves through the same strict, uncompromising discipline to ensure narrative integrity.

01 / 03 Pre
  1. Pre-Production
  2. Shoot
  3. Post
01

Pre-Production

Every project starts with absolute clarity of vision. We meticulously dissect the script, storyboard key frames, and scout environments long before a camera is built.

02

Shoot

Ideas earn their shape on set. Through precise lighting control, intentional directing, and dynamic cinematography, we capture the raw emotion required to build a compelling narrative.

03

Post

A film isn't finished until the rhythm is found. We cut, color grade, and mix audio until the final piece commands attention. We carry the footage through to the exact moment it meets the audience.

The Team

Behind the lens.

Founder & Director

Clifford Ray

Clifford is an award-winning Director and Content Creative with over nine years of industry expertise. Specializing in visual storytelling and full-scale production management, his portfolio spans international brands like BMW and Etihad Airways, as well as major esports organizations including EVOS and Nigma Galaxy.

Visual Artist

Razie Alfiedan

Razie is a Strategic Marketing and Communications Manager who brings over 14 years of experience blending creative direction with data-driven strategy. He orchestrates the complete content lifecycle, from storyboarding to post-production, spearheading high-impact campaigns for prominent brands.
