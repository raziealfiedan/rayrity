Our Pipeline

From script to screen.

Three stages, no shortcuts. Every Rayrity Films production moves through the same strict, uncompromising discipline to ensure narrative integrity.

01 / 03 Pre
  1. Pre-Production
  2. Shoot
  3. Post
01

Pre-Production

Every project starts with absolute clarity of vision. We meticulously dissect the script, storyboard key frames, and scout environments long before a camera is built. Preparation is where the film is truly authored.

02

Shoot

Ideas earn their shape on set. Through precise lighting control, intentional directing, and dynamic cinematography, we capture the raw emotion required to build a compelling narrative. Nothing is left to chance.

03

Post

A film isn't finished until the rhythm is found. We cut, color grade, and mix audio until the final piece commands attention. We carry the footage through to the exact moment it meets the audience.

The Team

Behind the lens.

Rayrity Films is guided by its creative leads, elevating every frame from the ground up.

Founder & Director

Clifford Ray

Clifford is an award-winning Director and Content Creative with over nine years of industry expertise. Specializing in visual storytelling and full-scale production management, his portfolio spans international brands like BMW and Etihad Airways, as well as major esports organizations including EVOS and Nigma Galaxy. His cinematic approach has earned him the Best Director nod at the BMW Shorties, and the Silver Award for the acclaimed documentary Sea Gypsy of Semporna.

Visual Artist

Razie Alfiedan

Razie is a Strategic Marketing and Communications Manager who brings over 14 years of experience blending creative direction with data-driven strategy. He orchestrates the complete content lifecycle—from storyboarding to post-production—spearheading high-impact campaigns for prominent brands across healthcare, hospitality, and real estate. Razie excels at translating complex brand narratives into engaging, highly optimized multimedia experiences.
